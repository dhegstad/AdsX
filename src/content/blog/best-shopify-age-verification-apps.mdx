Selling age-restricted products on Shopify requires an age verification gate between your storefront and the customer. Whether you sell alcohol, tobacco, vaping products, CBD, cannabis accessories, or certain supplements, failing to verify customer age exposes your business to legal liability, fines, and potential license revocation.

Shopify does not include native age verification functionality. You need a third-party app to display an age gate popup, collect date-of-birth information or ID verification, and restrict access to customers who do not meet the minimum age requirement. The best apps accomplish this while minimizing friction for legitimate customers and maintaining compliance with varying state and federal regulations.

This guide reviews the 7 best Shopify age verification apps, covering compliance features, customization options, and pricing.

## When Do You Need an Age Verification App?

Age verification is legally required for online sales of:

- **Alcohol**: All US states require age verification for online alcohol sales
- **Tobacco and vaping**: Federal law requires age verification for tobacco and nicotine products
- **Cannabis and CBD**: State-specific requirements, often including ID verification
- **Firearms and ammunition**: Federal requirements plus state-specific regulations
- **Adult content**: Industry-standard practice with varying legal requirements
- **Certain supplements**: Some jurisdictions restrict sale of specific supplements to minors

Even if your jurisdiction does not explicitly mandate an age gate, payment processors like Stripe and PayPal often require one as a condition of processing payments for restricted categories.

The app can verify age through date-of-birth entry, government ID upload and scanning, and third-party database checks against DMV and public records. For cannabis, alcohol, and tobacco stores that need to demonstrate robust compliance, AgeChecker provides audit trails and verification records that hold up to regulatory scrutiny. The app integrates with checkout to prevent age-restricted orders from processing without verification.

**Pricing**: $29/month for Standard; $79/month for Enhanced with ID verification; custom for Enterprise.

**Best for**: Stores in highly regulated categories (cannabis, alcohol) needing document-level age verification.

## How Do You Minimize the Conversion Impact of Age Verification?

Age gates inevitably add friction, but smart implementation reduces the impact:

**Match verification to risk level**: Use simple yes/no confirmation for low-risk categories and reserve ID verification for high-risk products. Over-verifying drives away legitimate customers without meaningful compliance benefit for most product types.

**Remember verified visitors**: Every app in this guide supports cookie-based return visitor recognition. Set cookies for at least 7 days so returning customers do not face the age gate repeatedly.

**Brand the experience**: A generic, ugly age gate popup signals an amateur store. Customize the popup to match your brand with your logo, brand colors, and messaging that feels intentional rather than bolted-on.

**Target specific pages**: If only some products in your catalog are age-restricted, apply the age gate to those specific collections or product pages rather than sitewide. Customers browsing non-restricted products should not encounter an age gate.

The right app handles compliance requirements while keeping friction as low as legally permitted.
