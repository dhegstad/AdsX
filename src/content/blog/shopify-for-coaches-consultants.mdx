## Setting Up Your Coaching Business on Shopify

Let's build your coaching business step by step.

### Step 1: Create Your Shopify Store

Start by visiting [Shopify](https://shopify.pxf.io/c/6318547/3797171/13624) to begin your free trial. During setup:

- Choose a store name that reflects your coaching brand
- Select your industry (services/professional services)
- Enter your business details and connect payment processing

### Step 2: Design Your Brand Experience

Your store should reflect your coaching philosophy and attract your ideal clients:

**Homepage Elements**:
- Clear headline communicating your transformation promise
- Professional photo or video introducing yourself
- Brief overview of who you help and how
- Prominent testimonials from past clients
- Clear calls-to-action for your main offerings

**About Page**:
- Your story and what led you to coaching
- Credentials, certifications, and experience
- Your coaching methodology and philosophy
- Media features and speaking engagements
- Professional photos that convey approachability

**Services/Programs Page**:
- Overview of all your offerings
- Clear progression from entry-level to premium
- Comparison tables for different program tiers
- Success stories for each program type

### Step 3: Create Your Coaching Products

Here's how to set up different coaching offerings as Shopify products:

**For Coaching Sessions/Packages**:

1. Go to Products > Add product
2. Enter package name (e.g., "6-Session Transformation Package")
3. Write compelling description covering:
   - Who this package is perfect for
   - What's included (sessions, support, resources)
   - Expected outcomes and transformations
   - Session format (video call, in-person, phone)
4. Set the price
5. Uncheck "This is a physical product" (critical step)
6. Set inventory to "Don't track inventory"
7. Add professional images representing the experience

**For Different Tiers**, use variants:
- Bronze: 4 sessions
- Silver: 8 sessions + email support
- Gold: 12 sessions + Voxer access + priority scheduling

## Booking Integration: Scheduling Your Sessions

Coaching businesses live and die by their calendar. Integrating booking functionality transforms how clients schedule with you.

**Acuity Scheduling**

The gold standard for coaches and consultants:
- Sophisticated intake forms capture information before sessions
- Package management tracks remaining sessions
- Native Zoom and Google Meet integration
- Time zone intelligence for global clients
- Automatic calendar sync
- Buffer time between appointments
- Payment collection at booking

**Sesami**

Excellent for coaches with teams or complex scheduling:
- Multiple staff member scheduling

- Automated reminders
- Custom booking questions

**Pricing**: From $19/month

**Calendly**

Simple and effective for straightforward needs:
- Clean, intuitive interface
- Round-robin scheduling for teams
- Embedding options for Shopify
- Integration with video conferencing

**Pricing**: Free plan available, premium from $10/month

### Setting Up Your Booking System

Follow these steps to integrate booking with your Shopify store:

1. **Install your chosen booking app** from the Shopify App Store
2. **Connect your calendar** (Google, Outlook, iCal)
3. **Define your availability**:
   - Working hours
   - Buffer time between sessions
   - Blocked dates (vacations, personal time)
   - Maximum appointments per day
4. **Create appointment types**:
   - Discovery calls (free or paid)
   - Individual coaching sessions
   - Strategy sessions
   - VIP days
5. **Set up intake forms**:
   - Client goals and challenges
   - Previous coaching experience
   - How they heard about you
   - Any relevant background information
6. **Configure reminders**:
   - 24-hour reminder email
   - 1-hour reminder with video link
7. **Enable payments** (optional):
   - Collect full payment at booking
   - Require deposits for premium sessions
   - Connect to Shopify for seamless tracking

### Embedding Booking on Product Pages

Most booking apps offer flexible integration options:

- **Embedded calendar**: Show available times directly on product pages
- **Book Now button**: Link to a dedicated scheduling page
- **Pop-up widget**: Floating booking button accessible from any page

For coaching packages with multiple sessions, clients typically purchase the package first, then receive booking access via email.

## Creating Coaching Packages That Sell

Strategic packaging transforms your coaching from hourly work into valuable programs.

### Pricing Architecture for Coaches

Design your offerings as a progression:

**Entry-Level: Single Sessions**
- Discovery call: Free or $50
- Single coaching session: $150-300
- Purpose: Let prospects experience your coaching

**Core: Coaching Packages**
- Starter Package: 4 sessions for $500 (save $100)
- Growth Package: 8 sessions for $900 (save $300)
- Transformation Package: 12 sessions for $1,200 (save $600)
- Purpose: Commit clients to the transformation process

**Premium: High-Ticket Programs**
- VIP Day: Full-day intensive for $2,000-5,000
- 90-Day Accelerator: Weekly sessions + daily support for $3,000-10,000
- Year-Long Partnership: Monthly sessions + ongoing support for $10,000+
- Purpose: Deep transformation with premium support

**Scalable: Group Programs**
- Group Coaching Cohort: 6 weeks with 10 participants for $500 each
- Mastermind: Monthly sessions with peers for $300/month
- Purpose: Serve more clients without trading more hours

### Package Components That Add Value

Beyond coaching sessions, include elements that enhance perceived value:

- **Session recordings**: Clients can revisit insights
- **Email support**: Questions answered between sessions
- **Voxer/Slack access**: Quick voice message support
- **Workbooks and templates**: Structured exercises to complete
- **Resource library**: Curated tools and resources
- **Priority scheduling**: First access to your calendar
- **Emergency sessions**: One unscheduled session for urgent needs
- **Certificate of completion**: Professional recognition

### Implementing Packages in Shopify

For each package:

1. Create a detailed product listing with everything included
2. Use bullet points for easy scanning
3. Show the value breakdown (e.g., "$1,800 value for $1,200")
4. Add testimonials specific to that package level
5. Include FAQ addressing common questions
6. Link to your booking system for session scheduling post-purchase

## Common Mistakes Coaches Make on Shopify

Avoid these pitfalls:

### Mistake 1: Vague Service Descriptions

"I help people live their best life" tells prospects nothing. Be specific about who you help, what problems you solve, and what outcomes you deliver.

### Mistake 2: No Social Proof

Trust is everything in coaching. If your site has no testimonials, case studies, or results, prospects have no reason to believe you can help them.

### Mistake 3: Complicated Booking Process

Every friction point in booking loses potential clients. Make scheduling as simple as possible—ideally two or three clicks from discovery to confirmation.

### Mistake 4: Only Offering Hourly Sessions

Trading time for money limits your income and impact. Create packages, courses, and memberships to scale beyond your available hours.

### Mistake 5: Ignoring Email Marketing

Your email list is your most valuable asset. Coaches who don't build and nurture an email list constantly struggle to fill programs.

### Mistake 6: No Clear Client Journey

Prospects should understand exactly what to do next. Create a clear path from free content to discovery call to paid program to premium services.
