Print on demand has become one of the most accessible ways to launch a product business on Shopify. You create designs, list products, and a print-on-demand partner handles production, inventory, and shipping when orders come in.

The model eliminates inventory risk entirely. You never purchase products upfront, never manage warehouse space, and never deal with unsold stock. Your only investment is in designs and marketing.

But not all POD apps are equal. The differences in product range, print quality, shipping speed, and pricing directly impact your customer experience and profit margins. Choosing the wrong POD partner means slow shipping, poor print quality, and customers who never return.

This guide compares the 10 best Shopify print-on-demand apps in 2026 across every metric that matters for building a profitable POD business.

## What Is the Best Overall Print-on-Demand App for Shopify?

### Printful

Printful is the most established POD app on [Shopify](https://shopify.pxf.io/c/6318547/3797171/13624) and remains the gold standard for print quality and brand experience. The product catalog includes 340+ items spanning apparel, accessories, home decor, and stationery, with new products added monthly.

Print quality on Printful is consistently rated highest among major POD providers. The company operates its own fulfillment centers rather than outsourcing to third-party printers, which gives them direct quality control over every order.

Custom branding options include printed inside labels, pack-ins, branded packing slips, and custom packaging sleeves. These features are essential for building a brand that feels premium rather than generic.

The tradeoff is price. Printful's base costs are among the highest in the POD space, which means tighter margins unless you position your products at premium price points.

**Best for**: Brands that prioritize print quality and customer experience over maximum margins.

### Printify

Printify takes a marketplace approach, connecting your Shopify store to a network of 80+ print providers worldwide. For each product, you can choose from multiple providers based on price, location, production time, and print quality.

This model gives you the largest product catalog in the POD space—900+ products—and the ability to optimize each product for different priorities. Need the cheapest t-shirt? Choose the lowest-cost provider. Need the fastest shipping to Europe? Choose an EU-based printer.

Printify Premium ($29.99/month) unlocks an additional 20% discount on all products, which significantly improves margins for stores processing high volumes.

The downside is inconsistency. Because different providers handle different products, print quality and shipping speed can vary between orders. Managing customer expectations requires more attention than with single-provider apps.

**Best for**: Stores that want maximum product variety and the ability to optimize costs across different providers.

### Gooten

Gooten operates a global manufacturing network with quality controls that balance consistency and competitive pricing. The product catalog of 250+ items focuses on the categories that sell best in POD: apparel, phone cases, home goods, and wall art.

Gooten's API-first approach makes it a strong choice for stores with custom integration needs. The platform also offers volume discounts that improve margins as your order count grows.

Custom branding includes neck labels, hang tags, and branded packaging available on select products. The branding options are more accessible than Printful's premium tier but less extensive.

**Best for**: Growing stores that need reliable quality at mid-range pricing with volume discount potential.

### SPOD

SPOD (Spreadshirt Print on Demand) is the speed champion of POD apps. The company guarantees 48-hour production on 95% of orders, making it the fastest POD provider by a significant margin.

If shipping speed is a competitive priority for your store, SPOD's production advantage translates to delivery times that rival traditional retail. When combined with expedited shipping, customers can receive POD products within 4-5 business days.

The product catalog of 200+ items covers core POD categories. Pricing is competitive, sitting between Printify's low end and Printful's premium tier.

**Best for**: Stores where fast delivery is a key competitive advantage and customer expectation.

### Gelato

Gelato operates the most extensive global fulfillment network, with production partners in 32 countries. Products are manufactured at the facility closest to the customer, reducing shipping distances and delivery times for international orders.

For stores with a global customer base, Gelato's local production model eliminates the international shipping problem that plagues most POD apps. A customer in Germany receives their order from a German facility. A customer in Japan receives from an Asian facility.

Gelato also emphasizes sustainability, using responsible sourcing and local production to minimize the carbon footprint of each order—a selling point for eco-conscious brands.

**Best for**: International stores selling to customers worldwide that need fast, affordable global delivery.

### CustomCat

CustomCat processes orders quickly (2-3 day production) and offers competitive base pricing across 550+ products. The product range includes unique items not available on other platforms, including premium embroidered products and specialized sublimation items.

CustomCat's pricing structure rewards volume with lower per-unit costs as your monthly orders increase. The company focuses exclusively on US fulfillment, which limits international shipping options but delivers fast domestic service.

**Best for**: US-focused stores that need competitive pricing with a large product selection.

## Which POD Apps Serve Specialized Niches?

### Teelaunch

Teelaunch offers a curated product catalog focused on trending and unique items. The selection includes standard apparel and accessories plus specialty products like jewelry, pet products, and seasonal items.

Pricing is among the lowest in the POD space, making Teelaunch attractive for stores competing on price or testing new product ideas with minimal financial risk.

**Best for**: Budget-conscious stores and merchants testing new POD product ideas.

### Lulu Direct

Lulu Direct is the go-to POD app for books, journals, notebooks, and printed materials. If your Shopify store sells self-published books, branded journals, educational materials, or custom planners, Lulu Direct handles the printing and fulfillment.

The platform supports hardcover, softcover, and spiral-bound formats with professional print quality. Integration with Shopify is seamless, with real-time inventory sync and automated order fulfillment.

**Best for**: Authors, educators, coaches, and brands selling printed books, journals, or educational materials.

### Prodigi

Prodigi specializes in premium print products including fine art prints, canvas wraps, photo books, and high-end wall decor. The print quality on Prodigi's art products is noticeably superior to general POD providers.

The global fulfillment network covers major markets with local production. Prodigi is the preferred choice for artists, photographers, and brands selling wall art and premium home decor.

**Best for**: Artists, photographers, and stores selling premium wall art and home decor products.

### AOP+ (All-Over Print)

AOP+ specializes in all-over print products where your design covers the entire product surface rather than a single print area. All-over print apparel, leggings, dresses, and accessories offer a premium, distinctive look impossible with standard printing methods.

The all-over print process requires cut-and-sew manufacturing, which means longer production times (3-7 days) and higher base costs. But the resulting products command premium prices and stand out from standard POD offerings.

**Best for**: Fashion-focused brands that want distinctive all-over print apparel and accessories.

## How Should You Choose a POD App for Your Shopify Store?

Start by ordering samples from your top 2-3 candidates. No amount of research replaces holding the actual product in your hands. Evaluate print quality, fabric feel, packaging presentation, and the unboxing experience from a customer's perspective.

Consider these priorities in order:

1. **Print quality**: The foundation of customer satisfaction and repeat purchases
2. **Product range**: Ensure the app offers the specific products you plan to sell
3. **Shipping speed**: Match delivery expectations for your target market
4. **Pricing**: Calculate actual margins per product after base cost, shipping, and transaction fees
5. **Branding options**: Custom labels, packaging, and inserts for brand building

Most successful POD stores use 2-3 providers rather than committing to a single app. Use Printful for premium products where quality matters most, Printify for commodity items where price competition is fierce, and a specialist like Lulu Direct or Prodigi for niche product categories.
