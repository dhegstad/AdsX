## What AI Analyzes in Fashion Products

Understanding what AI evaluates helps you optimize effectively.

### 1. Material Composition and Quality Signals

AI places heavy weight on specific material information:

**Fabric details:**
- Exact fiber composition (e.g., "65% organic cotton, 35% recycled polyester")
- Fabric weight (e.g., "12 oz heavyweight denim")
- Weave or knit type (e.g., "French terry", "twill", "jersey knit")
- Treatment or finish (e.g., "garment-dyed", "stonewashed", "pre-shrunk")

**Quality indicators:**
- Thread count for cotton items
- Ply count for knits
- GSM (grams per square meter) for fabrics
- Origin of materials (e.g., "Japanese selvedge denim", "Italian leather")

**Example of what AI prefers:**

*Vague (poor for AI):*
> "Premium cotton hoodie with soft feel"

*Specific (good for AI):*
> "Heavyweight 12 oz hoodie made from 100% organic cotton French terry (350 GSM). Pre-shrunk, garment-dyed for softness. Internally brushed fleece lining."

### 2. Sizing and Fit Information

AI matches products to fit-specific queries using detailed sizing data:

| Fit Query Type | What AI Looks For |
|---------------|------------------|
| Body type specific | "Athletic fit", "curvy fit", "petite sizing" |
| Fit style | "Relaxed", "slim", "oversized", "tailored" |
| Measurements | Exact measurements for each size in inches/cm |
| Fit guidance | "Runs large", "true to size", "size up for oversized fit" |
| Height recommendations | "Best for 5'4" to 5'8"", inseam lengths |

**Comprehensive size guide example:**

```
Size M:
- Chest: 40-42"
- Waist: 32-34"
- Length: 28"
- Shoulder: 18"
- Sleeve: 34"
- Model height: 6'0" wearing size M
- Fit: True to size with room for layering
```

## Common Mistakes Fashion Brands Make

### Mistake 1: Vague Material Descriptions

**Problem:** "Premium cotton blend" tells AI nothing specific.

**Fix:** "68% Pima cotton, 29% recycled polyester, 3% spandex - 220 GSM single jersey knit"

### Mistake 2: Insufficient Sizing Information

**Problem:** Only providing S/M/L/XL without measurements or fit guidance.

**Fix:** Provide exact measurements for every size, multiple data points, fit guidance, and model stats.

### Mistake 3: Generic Product Titles

**Problem:** "Women's Jeans" makes it impossible for AI to understand what differentiates your product.

**Fix:** "High-Rise Straight Leg Jeans in Vintage Black Wash - 100% Organic Cotton (30" Inseam)"

### Mistake 4: Missing Care Instructions

**Problem:** Shoppers want to know how to care for items before purchase.

**Fix:** Include detailed care instructions: "Machine wash cold, gentle cycle. Hang dry or tumble dry low. Iron on low heat if needed. Do not bleach or dry clean."

### Mistake 5: Ignoring Fit Feedback in Reviews

**Problem:** Reviews say "runs small" but you don't address it in product description.

**Fix:** Update product info: "Note: Based on customer feedback, this style runs slightly small. We recommend sizing up if between sizes or prefer a relaxed fit."

### Mistake 6: No Sustainability Information

**Problem:** Modern consumers actively seek sustainable optionsâ€”if you don't share your story, AI can't recommend you for those queries.

**Fix:** Detail materials, production methods, certifications, and environmental impact even if you're early in sustainability journey.

### Mistake 7: Inconsistent Information Across Platforms

**Problem:** Different material compositions or sizing listed on your site vs. Amazon vs. Nordstrom.

**Fix:** Maintain a single source of truth for all product data. Audit platforms quarterly for consistency.
