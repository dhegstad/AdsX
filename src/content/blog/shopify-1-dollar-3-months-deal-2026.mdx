- Upload your logo
- Set your brand colors and fonts
- Write your "About Us" page
- Create a contact page with a form

### Product Phase (Week 2-4)

With your foundation in place, it is time to add products:

**For Physical Products:**
- Upload high-quality product photos (aim for at least 4-5 per product)
- Write compelling product descriptions that focus on benefits, not just features
- Set up variants for different sizes, colors, and options
- Configure inventory tracking
- Set your pricing with appropriate margins

**For Dropshipping:**
- Connect a supplier app like DSers, Spocket, or Zendrop
- Import products from your supplier
- Customize product titles and descriptions (never use the default supplier copy)
- Set competitive pricing with healthy margins (aim for 30-50%)

**For Digital Products:**
- Upload your digital files
- Set up automatic delivery with an app like Digital Downloads or Sky Pilot
- Create product previews and mockups
- Write clear delivery instructions

**For Print on Demand:**
- Connect a POD provider like Printful, Printify, or Gooten
- Design your products using the provider's mockup generator
- Create product listings with lifestyle mockup images
- Set pricing that covers base cost plus your desired margin

### Launch Phase (Week 4-6)

Your store is stocked and configured. Now it is time to open for business:

**Pre-Launch Checklist:**
- Place a test order to verify your checkout works

- Check your store on mobile devices
- Ask friends or family to review the user experience

**Launch Marketing:**
- Post your store on your personal social media accounts
- Set up a Google Merchant Center account for free product listings
- Create your first email campaign for friends and family
- Consider a small Facebook or Instagram ad budget ($5-10/day)
- Share in relevant online communities (Reddit, Facebook groups)

### Growth Phase (Week 6-12)

With your store live and orders coming in, shift to growth:

**Optimize Conversions:**
- Review your Shopify analytics to identify drop-off points
- A/B test your product pages (different images, descriptions, CTAs)

- Add social proof — customer reviews, testimonials, user-generated content
- Optimize your checkout with trust badges and clear shipping information

**Scale Marketing:**
- Double down on channels that drive sales
- Start building an email list with pop-ups and exit-intent offers
- Launch retargeting ads to visitors who did not purchase
- Explore influencer partnerships for product promotion
- Start a blog to drive organic search traffic

**Expand Revenue:**
- Add upsell and cross-sell apps
- Create product bundles
- Set up subscription options for consumable products
