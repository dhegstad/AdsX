Forms are the connective tissue between your Shopify store and your customers beyond the buy button. Custom order requests, wholesale inquiries, product personalization specs, warranty claims, event registrations, and feedback collection all require forms that Shopify's native contact page cannot handle.

Shopify's built-in contact form collects four fixed fields and emails submissions to your inbox. No custom fields, no conditional logic, no file uploads, no integrations, no submission dashboard. For stores that need forms to drive business processes — not just collect messages — a dedicated form builder app is essential.

This guide reviews the 8 best Shopify form builder apps, covering field types, logic capabilities, integrations, and pricing.

## What Can a Form Builder App Do That Shopify Cannot?

Dedicated form builders unlock capabilities that Shopify's native form lacks:

- **Custom fields**: Dropdowns, checkboxes, radio buttons, date pickers, file uploads, and calculated fields
- **Conditional logic**: Show or hide fields based on previous answers (selecting "wholesale" reveals quantity and business fields)
- **Multi-step forms**: Break long forms into pages with progress indicators to reduce abandonment
- **Integrations**: Send submissions to Klaviyo, Mailchimp, HubSpot, Google Sheets, Slack, or Zapier
- **Submission management**: Dashboard for reviewing, filtering, and exporting form submissions without digging through email
- **Auto-responses**: Automatic confirmation emails to customers upon form submission

## What Are the Best Shopify Form Builder Apps?

### 1. Hulk Form Builder (Top Pick for Custom Forms)

Hulk Form Builder is the most capable form builder on Shopify, supporting advanced conditional logic, calculated fields, file uploads, and product-linked fields. The visual builder uses drag-and-drop to create forms of any complexity — from simple contact forms to multi-page custom order forms with dynamic pricing calculations. Conditional logic lets you create branching paths where different field selections reveal different subsequent fields, keeping forms relevant and concise. Every submission is stored in a searchable dashboard with export capabilities, and the app integrates natively with Klaviyo, Mailchimp, HubSpot, and Slack.

**Pricing**: Free for 5 forms with basic fields; $9.90/month for conditional logic and file uploads; $19.90/month for unlimited with calculated fields.

**Best for**: Stores needing complex custom forms with logic, calculations, and file uploads.

### 2. Powerful Contact Form

Powerful Contact Form enhances Shopify's basic contact form with custom fields, Google reCAPTCHA spam protection, auto-response emails, and submission tracking. The app replaces the default contact page form with a more capable version while maintaining familiar placement and styling. Custom field types include dropdowns, checkboxes, date pickers, and text areas. The spam protection is particularly effective — reCAPTCHA v3 runs invisibly without requiring customers to solve puzzles. Submissions are viewable in the app dashboard and automatically forwarded to designated email addresses.

**Pricing**: Free for basic form; $5.99/month for custom fields; $9.99/month for file uploads and auto-responses.

**Best for**: Stores that want an upgraded contact form with spam protection without building forms from scratch.

---

### 3. Jotform

Jotform is a dedicated form building platform (not Shopify-exclusive) that integrates with Shopify stores through its app. The advantage is Jotform's massive feature set: 10,000+ form templates, 100+ integrations, payment collection through forms, e-signature fields, PDF report generation, and approval workflows. For complex use cases like wholesale applications, custom product configurators, or event registration with payment, Jotform provides capabilities that Shopify-native form apps cannot match. The tradeoff is a more complex interface and forms that may not match your Shopify theme as seamlessly.

**Pricing**: Free for up to 5 forms and 100 submissions/month; $39/month for 25 forms and 1,000 submissions.

**Best for**: Stores with complex form requirements including payment collection, e-signatures, or approval workflows.

### 4. Formilla

Formilla combines contact forms with live chat and a help desk system. When customers submit a form, the submission creates a support ticket that your team can respond to through Formilla's dashboard. The live chat component lets customers switch from form submission to real-time conversation when immediate help is available. Formilla's forms include basic custom fields and auto-response functionality. The combined approach works well for stores where form submissions frequently lead to back-and-forth conversations — custom order inquiries, support requests, and pre-sale questions.

**Pricing**: Free for 1 agent; $13.99/month for Pro; $29.99/month for Growth with advanced features.

**Best for**: Stores that need forms integrated with live chat and customer support workflows.

### 5. JEPP Forms

JEPP Forms provides simple custom forms with email routing — different form submissions go to different email addresses based on form type or field selections. The app supports standard field types (text, email, dropdown, checkbox, textarea) with clean, minimal design. JEPP's standout feature is multi-department routing: a "Reason for Contact" dropdown that sends wholesale inquiries to your sales team, support requests to your help desk, and press inquiries to your marketing team. Setup takes under 10 minutes.

**Pricing**: Free for 1 form; $3.49/month for 5 forms; $6.99/month for unlimited.

**Best for**: Stores that need to route different form submissions to different teams or departments.

Happy Contact Form focuses on visual design, creating contact forms that match any Shopify theme with pixel-perfect styling. The app offers multiple layout templates (inline, sidebar, floating button, popup) and extensive styling controls for colors, fonts, borders, spacing, and animations. Happy Contact Form includes custom fields, Google Maps embeds for location-based businesses, and social media links within the form widget. The design-first approach makes it the best choice for stores where brand aesthetics extend to every customer touchpoint.

**Best for**: Design-conscious stores where form appearance must match brand aesthetics exactly.

Qikify creates multiple form types including contact forms, order forms, feedback forms, and wholesale application forms with popup and embedded display options. The popup trigger system lets you display forms based on exit intent, time delay, scroll depth, or button click. Qikify's form analytics track submission rates, abandonment points, and completion times to help you optimize form performance. The app supports conditional fields and multi-step layouts on paid plans. The combination of form building and smart triggering makes it effective for capturing leads at the right moment.

**Pricing**: Free for 1 form; $5.99/month for 5 forms with popups; $9.99/month for unlimited with analytics.

**Best for**: Stores wanting forms with smart popup triggers and conversion analytics.

### 8. powr Forms

powr Forms includes a payment field type that lets you collect payments directly through form submissions — useful for custom orders, deposits, service bookings, and donation collection. The drag-and-drop builder supports 20+ field types including signature capture, star ratings, and image choice fields. powr integrates with Mailchimp, Google Sheets, and Zapier for submission routing. The payment integration works with PayPal and Stripe, turning any form into a checkout-capable endpoint. For stores that need to collect payment alongside custom information, powr eliminates the need for a separate checkout process.

**Pricing**: Free for basic forms; $5.49/month for custom fields; $11.99/month for payment fields and integrations.

**Best for**: Stores that need to collect payment through custom forms for orders, deposits, or services.

## How Should You Set Up Forms on Your Shopify Store?

Three guidelines for effective form implementation:

**Keep forms as short as possible**: Every additional field reduces completion rates by approximately 5-10%. Only ask for information you will actually use. If a field is not essential, remove it or make it optional. Use conditional logic to hide fields that are irrelevant based on previous selections.

**Route submissions intelligently**: Do not send all form submissions to a single inbox. Use email routing or integrations to direct wholesale inquiries to sales, support requests to your help desk, and feedback to your product team. Response time drops dramatically when the right person sees the submission immediately.

**Set up auto-responses**: Customers who submit a form expect acknowledgment. Configure automatic confirmation emails that tell the customer their submission was received, provide a reference number, and set expectations for response time. This reduces follow-up "did you get my form?" messages and builds trust.
