Once you've selected a theme, customize it for your beauty brand:

## Setting Up Shade Matching and Product Variants

One of the biggest challenges for beauty brands on Shopify is handling extensive shade ranges and product variations.

### Understanding Shopify's Variant Limitations

Shopify allows up to 100 variants per product and 3 variant options (e.g., Shade, Size, Finish). For most beauty products, this is sufficient:

**Example: Foundation with 40 shades in 2 sizes**
- 40 shades x 2 sizes = 80 variants (under the 100 limit)
- Works within standard Shopify functionality

**Challenge: Foundation with 60 shades in 3 sizes**
- 60 shades x 3 sizes = 180 variants (exceeds 100 limit)
- Requires creative solutions

### Solutions for Large Shade Ranges

**Option 1: Separate products by shade family**

Group shades into collections:
- Fair Shades (Shades 1-20)
- Medium Shades (Shades 21-40)
- Deep Shades (Shades 41-60)

Each becomes its own product with manageable variants.

**Option 2: Use variant extension apps**

Apps like **Infinite Options** or **Hulk Product Options** extend Shopify's native variant system:
- Add unlimited custom options
- Conditional logic (show shade 2.5 only if "warm undertone" selected)
- Visual swatch displays

**Option 3: Separate products, unified display**

Create separate SKUs per shade but display them together using:
- Combined product pages via app
- Collection page with swatch navigation
- Quick-add from collection grid

### Implementing Visual Shade Swatches

Standard Shopify dropdown menus don't work for shade selection. Customers need to see the actual colors.

**Using theme swatch functionality:**
Most premium themes include swatch support. Upload swatch images named to match your variant names:
- `fair-ivory.png`
- `light-beige.png`
- `medium-tan.png`

**Using swatch apps:**
Apps like **Variant Image Automator** or **Swatchy** automate swatch display:
- Pull swatches from product images
- Show variant images on hover
- Group by undertone or finish

### Shade Matching Technology Integration

For inclusive shade ranges, AI-powered shade matching reduces returns and increases conversion:

**Implementation approach:**

1. **Photography standards**: Photograph all shades on diverse skin tones under consistent lighting

2. **Shade descriptions**: Write detailed descriptions including:
   - Undertone (warm, neutral, cool)
   - Depth (fair, light, medium, tan, deep, dark)
   - Best for skin types

3. **Quiz integration**: Build "find your shade" quiz asking about:
   - Current foundation matches from other brands
   - Undertone indicators (vein color, jewelry preference)
   - Skin concerns
   - Coverage preference

4. **AR try-on**: Integrate Perfect Corp or similar for virtual try-on using device camera

## Creating Irresistible Product Bundles

Bundles are essential for beauty e-commerce. They increase average order value, introduce customers to new products, and create perceived value.

### Types of Beauty Bundles

**Routine bundles:**
- "Complete Skincare Routine" - Cleanser + Toner + Serum + Moisturizer
- "Morning Essentials" - SPF + Vitamin C + Moisturizer
- "Night Repair Set" - Cleansing Oil + Retinol + Night Cream

**Value sets:**
- "Bestseller Collection" - Three top sellers at discount
- "Try Me Set" - Mini sizes of hero products
- "Holiday Gift Set" - Full sizes with bonus mini

**Complementary bundles:**
- "Perfect Pair" - Foundation + Matching Concealer
- "Lip Kit" - Liner + Lipstick + Gloss
- "Eye Look" - Eyeshadow Palette + Mascara + Liner

**Replenishment bundles:**
- "3-Month Supply" - Three of the same product
- "Subscription Starter" - First delivery with bonus product
- "Stock Up & Save" - Volume discount on favorites

### Bundle Pricing Strategies

**Percentage discount:**
- "Save 20% when you buy the routine"
- Simple to understand
- Works for most bundles

**Fixed savings:**
- "Save $25 on this set"
- Feels more substantial for higher-priced bundles
- Clear value proposition

**Free product positioning:**
- "Buy 2, Get 1 Free"
- "Free travel size with purchase"
- Psychologically powerful

**Tiered bundling:**
- Spend $50: 10% off
- Spend $100: 15% off
- Spend $150: 20% off + free gift

### Building Bundles in Shopify

**Pre-built bundles:**

Create bundles as their own products:
1. Create new product with bundle name
2. Set price at discounted total
3. Use inventory sync app to deduct from component products
4. Show savings prominently in description

**Build-your-own bundles:**

Use Bundle Builder or PickyStory apps:
1. Set bundle rules (pick 4 products from this collection)
2. Configure discount (20% off when bundle complete)
3. Add visual progress indicator
4. Confirm bundle contents before checkout

**Mix-and-match bundles:**

Perfect for shade-intensive categories:
1. Customer picks 3 lipsticks from any shade
2. Bundle discount applies at 3+
3. Works great for "build your lip wardrobe" promotions

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Poor Product Photography

Beauty is visual. Low-quality images kill conversion. Invest in professional photography showing:
- Products on various skin tones
- Texture and finish details
- Swatches and shade accuracy
- Application and results

### 2. Incomplete Shade Information

Customers can't touch products online. Provide:
- Detailed undertone descriptions
- Shade comparison guides
- Diverse model imagery
- Virtual try-on where possible

### 3. Ignoring Mobile Experience

Most beauty shopping happens on mobile. Test every change on mobile devices. Ensure swatch selection, checkout, and payment work flawlessly.

### 4. Neglecting Post-Purchase

The sale isn't the end. Beauty customers need:
- How-to instructions
- Routine integration guidance
- Replenishment reminders
- Incentives to review

### 5. Underinvesting in UGC

Brand photography isn't enough. Customers want to see products on real people. Build systems to collect and display user content.
