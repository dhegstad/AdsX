This comprehensive checklist covers everything you need to optimize your brand's visibility in AI-powered search. Use it as a roadmap for your AI search optimization (AISO) efforts.

**Bookmark this page** — we update it regularly as AI platforms evolve.

---

## Foundation: Brand Information

### Website Basics

- [ ] **Clear brand description** on homepage explaining what you do in 1-2 sentences
- [ ] **Consistent brand name** used across all pages (exact spelling, capitalization)
- [ ] **Founded date and company history** clearly stated
- [ ] **Pricing information** current and easily accessible
- [ ] **Product/service descriptions** with specific features and benefits
- [ ] **Target audience** clearly defined ("built for X" or "designed for Y")

### Structured Data

- [ ] **Organization schema** with name, description, URL, logo, founding date
- [ ] **Product/SoftwareApplication schema** for each product
- [ ] **FAQ schema** on relevant pages
- [ ] **Review/Rating schema** if applicable
- [ ] **Breadcrumb schema** for navigation hierarchy
- [ ] **Article schema** on blog posts

### Information Consistency

- [ ] **Google Business Profile** matches website information
- [ ] **LinkedIn company page** has accurate, current information
- [ ] **Crunchbase profile** (if applicable) is up to date
- [ ] **Social media profiles** have consistent brand information
- [ ] **Wikipedia article** (if notable enough) is accurate

## Content Optimization

### Definitional Content

- [ ] **"What is [Your Product]?"** page with clear explanation
- [ ] **"How [Your Product] works"** page with detailed breakdown
- [ ] **"[Your Product] vs alternatives"** comparison page
- [ ] **"Who uses [Your Product]?"** page with use cases
- [ ] **"[Your Product] pricing"** page with clear tiers

### Question-Based Content

- [ ] **FAQ page** covering common customer questions
- [ ] **Individual FAQ items** using actual questions as headers
- [ ] **"How to" guides** for common tasks in your category
- [ ] **Buying guides** for your product category
- [ ] **Category explainer** (e.g., "What is CRM software?")

### Use Case Content

- [ ] **Industry-specific pages** (e.g., "CRM for healthcare")
- [ ] **Company size pages** (e.g., "CRM for small business")
- [ ] **Role-specific pages** (e.g., "CRM for sales teams")
- [ ] **Problem-specific pages** (e.g., "CRM for managing leads")

## Authority Building

### Media and PR

- [ ] **Press page** with media coverage and press releases
- [ ] **At least 3 mentions** in authoritative industry publications
- [ ] **Founder/executive thought leadership** articles published
- [ ] **Podcast appearances** or interviews (transcripts available)
- [ ] **News coverage** for major announcements

### Review Sites

- [ ] **G2 profile** complete with current information (B2B)
- [ ] **Capterra profile** complete with current information (B2B)
- [ ] **Trustpilot profile** with customer reviews (B2C)
- [ ] **Industry-specific review sites** (e.g., Product Hunt for tech)
- [ ] **20+ reviews** on primary review platforms

### Third-Party Content

- [ ] **Listed in "best of" roundup articles** in your category
- [ ] **Included in comparison articles** (vs. competitors)
- [ ] **Mentioned in educational resources** (courses, tutorials)
- [ ] **Cited in research or reports** relevant to your industry

## Technical Optimization

### Site Performance

- [ ] **Mobile-friendly** design (responsive)
- [ ] **Fast loading** (under 3 seconds)
- [ ] **Secure** (HTTPS)
- [ ] **Accessible** (WCAG compliance)
- [ ] **No broken links** or errors

### Crawlability

- [ ] **XML sitemap** submitted to search engines
- [ ] **Robots.txt** allows AI crawlers (Googlebot, Bingbot, ChatGPT-User, etc.)
- [ ] **Clean URL structure** (descriptive, hierarchical)
- [ ] **Internal linking** connects related content
- [ ] **No orphan pages** (all pages linked from navigation or sitemap)

### Content Formatting

- [ ] **Clear heading hierarchy** (H1, H2, H3) on all pages
- [ ] **Bullet points and lists** for scannable content
- [ ] **Tables** for comparative information
- [ ] **Bold/emphasis** on key terms and phrases
- [ ] **Quotable statements** (clear, definitive sentences AI can cite)

## AI Platform Specific

### ChatGPT Optimization

- [ ] **Tested queries** — asked ChatGPT about your category
- [ ] **Documented current visibility** — how you appear today
- [ ] **Identified gaps** — where competitors are mentioned but you aren't
- [ ] **ChatGPT Ads** — evaluated sponsored placement opportunities

### Perplexity Optimization

- [ ] **Tested queries** — searched your category on Perplexity
- [ ] **Source citations** — checked if your site is cited as source
- [ ] **Perplexity Ads** — evaluated sponsored answer opportunities
- [ ] **Content freshness** — ensured recent content for RAG retrieval

### Claude Optimization

- [ ] **Tested queries** — asked Claude about your category
- [ ] **Documented representation** — noted accuracy and sentiment
- [ ] **Compared to competitors** — benchmarked your visibility

### Google AI Overviews

- [ ] **Tested queries** — checked Google AI Overviews in your category
- [ ] **SGE optimization** — ensured content structured for featured snippets
- [ ] **Source visibility** — checked if you appear as cited source

## Monitoring and Measurement

### Regular Audits

- [ ] **Monthly AI query audit** — test 10+ queries across platforms
- [ ] **Track mention rate** — percentage of queries mentioning you
- [ ] **Track sentiment** — positive, negative, or neutral representation
- [ ] **Track position** — first mentioned, or buried in list?
- [ ] **Track accuracy** — is information about you correct?

### Competitive Intelligence

- [ ] **Competitor AI audit** — how do competitors appear?
- [ ] **Share of voice** — your mentions vs. competitor mentions
- [ ] **Differentiation** — does AI clearly distinguish you from competitors?

### Documentation

- [ ] **Baseline report** — document current state before optimization
- [ ] **Monthly progress reports** — track changes over time
- [ ] **Screenshot evidence** — save AI responses for records

## Quick Reference: Priority Actions

### If You're Just Starting (Do First)

1. Complete website basics section
2. Add structured data (Organization, Product, FAQ schemas)
3. Create definitional content ("What is X" pages)
4. Audit current AI visibility
5. Ensure review site profiles are complete

### If You're Intermediate (Do Next)

1. Build out use case content
2. Pursue PR and media coverage
3. Create comparison content
4. Implement technical optimizations
5. Set up monthly monitoring

### If You're Advanced (Do Last)

1. Explore AI advertising options
2. Build community/ecosystem features
3. Publish original research
4. Develop advanced thought leadership
5. Create integration/partner content
