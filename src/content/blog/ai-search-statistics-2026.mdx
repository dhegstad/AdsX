## Search Behavior Shifts

### Zero-Click and AI Search

15. **65% of Google searches** result in zero clicks (SparkToro, 2025)

16. **40% of Gen Z** prefers AI assistants or social media over traditional search (GWI, 2025)

17. **28% of all US adults** have used an AI assistant for product research (Pew, 2025)

18. **73% of AI assistant users** trust AI recommendations without additional verification (consumer survey, 2025)

19. **52% of users** who receive AI recommendations don't visit any website afterward (behavioral study, 2025)

### Query Patterns

20. **4.2x longer** queries are asked to AI assistants vs. traditional search (avg. 23 words vs. 5.5 words)

21. **67% of AI queries** are phrased as questions vs. 32% in traditional search

22. **83% of users** ask follow-up questions after initial AI response

23. **Top query categories** for AI assistants: How-to (34%), Recommendations (28%), Explanations (22%), Comparisons (16%)

37. **Featured snippet traffic** down 8% since AI summaries expanded (Ahrefs, 2025)

38. **Long-tail keyword traffic** most impacted by AI search (-18% average)

39. **Brand searches increase 23%** on average when brands are mentioned by AI (brand study)

40. **AI mentions correlate with** 31% higher brand recall vs. traditional search results

41. **65% of consumers** say AI recommendation makes them more likely to consider a brand
