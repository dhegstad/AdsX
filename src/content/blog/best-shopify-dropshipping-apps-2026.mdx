Dropshipping remains the lowest-barrier entry point into e-commerce. You list products, customers order them, and a third-party supplier ships directly to the buyer. No warehouse, no inventory risk, no upfront product investment. The entire model hinges on one thing: the app connecting your Shopify store to reliable suppliers with reasonable shipping times.

The dropshipping app landscape has evolved well beyond basic AliExpress importers. Modern apps offer US and EU supplier networks, automated order fulfillment, branded packaging, print-on-demand integration, and real-time inventory syncing. The gap between a mediocre and excellent dropshipping app is the difference between 3-day and 30-day shipping — and that gap determines whether customers come back.

This guide reviews the 10 best Shopify dropshipping apps, covering supplier networks, shipping speeds, automation features, and pricing.

## What Should You Look for in a Dropshipping App?

The five factors that separate good dropshipping apps from bad ones:

- **Supplier quality**: Vetted suppliers with consistent product quality and reliable fulfillment
- **Shipping speed**: US/EU warehouse options for domestic delivery under 7 days
- **Automation depth**: Auto-ordering, tracking sync, inventory updates, and price change alerts
- **Product variety**: Broad catalog across categories with competitive wholesale pricing
- **Branding options**: Custom packaging, inserts, and branded invoices to build repeat business

## What Are the Best Shopify Dropshipping Apps?

### 1. DSers (Top Pick for AliExpress Dropshipping)

DSers is the official AliExpress dropshipping partner for Shopify and the direct successor to Oberlo. The app handles product importing, variant mapping, bulk ordering, and automatic tracking updates with a polished interface that simplifies AliExpress sourcing. DSers' supplier optimizer automatically finds cheaper or higher-rated suppliers for products you already sell. The bulk order feature lets you process hundreds of orders simultaneously rather than one at a time, which is critical at scale.

**Pricing**: Free for 3 stores and 3,000 products; $19.90/month for Advanced; $49.90/month for Pro.

**Best for**: Stores sourcing primarily from AliExpress that need efficient bulk ordering.

---

### 2. Spocket

Spocket differentiates itself by curating suppliers primarily from the US and EU, which translates to shipping times that AliExpress-based apps cannot match. Domestic orders typically arrive in 2-5 business days. Spocket vets every supplier, and the product catalog includes branded invoicing so customers see your brand, not the supplier's. The app integrates with Shopify's order system for one-click fulfillment, and real-time inventory sync prevents overselling products that go out of stock at the supplier level.

**Pricing**: $39.99/month for Starter; $59.99/month for Pro with branded invoicing; $99.99/month for Empire.

**Best for**: Stores targeting US/EU customers who expect fast shipping and consistent quality.

---

### 3. Zendrop

Zendrop combines AliExpress-level product variety with US warehouse fulfillment for top-selling items. The platform's standout feature is custom branding — even on the free plan, you can add branded packaging, thank-you cards, and custom inserts to orders. Zendrop's subscription box feature automates recurring shipments for subscription-based dropshipping models. The supplier vetting process is thorough, and the platform handles returns and refunds on your behalf for qualifying products.

**Pricing**: Free for basic; $49/month for Pro with branding and faster processing.

**Best for**: Stores building a branded experience despite using dropshipping fulfillment.

---

### 4. CJDropshipping

CJDropshipping operates its own global warehouse network with facilities in the US, EU, and Southeast Asia. This gives the platform control over shipping speed that pure marketplace connectors lack. CJ offers product sourcing — if you find a product anywhere online, their team will source it and list it in your CJ account. The app also supports print-on-demand, custom packaging, and product photography services. The free pricing model charges only per order with no monthly subscription.

**Pricing**: Free to use; you pay product cost + shipping per order.

**Best for**: Stores wanting diverse sourcing options with warehouse fulfillment.

---

### 5. AutoDS

AutoDS provides the deepest automation stack in the dropshipping category. The platform monitors supplier prices and stock levels 24/7, automatically adjusting your store's pricing and availability. AutoDS supports sourcing from over 25 suppliers including AliExpress, Amazon, Walmart, and CJDropshipping. The automatic order fulfillment feature processes orders without manual intervention, and the product research tool identifies trending products based on sales velocity data.

**Pricing**: $26.90/month for Import 200; $44.90/month for Starter 500; scales up to enterprise.

**Best for**: Stores prioritizing full automation to minimize daily operational time.

---

### 6. Printful

Printful is the leading print-on-demand platform, enabling stores to sell custom-designed apparel, accessories, and home goods with zero inventory. You upload designs, Printful prints and ships each order on demand. The product catalog includes over 340 products from t-shirts and hoodies to phone cases and wall art. Printful operates production facilities in the US, EU, and Mexico, ensuring fast regional fulfillment. The mockup generator creates professional product images instantly.

**Pricing**: Free to connect; you pay production cost per item (retail minus production = your margin).

**Best for**: Stores selling custom-designed or branded merchandise without holding inventory.

---

### 7. Syncee

Syncee focuses on connecting Shopify stores with vetted, brand-name suppliers from the US, EU, and globally. Unlike AliExpress-focused apps, Syncee's supplier network includes established distributors and manufacturers offering authentic branded products. The automated product data sync keeps prices, descriptions, and stock levels current across your catalog. Syncee's marketplace lets you browse supplier catalogs and import products with pre-negotiated wholesale pricing.

**Pricing**: Free for up to 25 products; $29/month for 500 products; $79/month for 10,000; $129/month for unlimited.

**Best for**: Stores wanting to sell established brand-name products through dropshipping.

---

### 8. Modalyst

Modalyst curates a premium supplier network featuring independent brands, sustainable products, and brand-name goods. The platform is an official Shopify dropshipping partner and emphasizes product quality over the lowest possible price. Modalyst's US supplier network delivers in 3-8 business days domestically. The app automatically calculates your profit margin at import so you can evaluate products before listing them. Luxury and premium product categories are better represented than on AliExpress-based platforms.

**Pricing**: Free for up to 25 products; $35/month for 250 products; $90/month for unlimited.

**Best for**: Stores targeting premium market segments with higher-quality products.

---

### 9. Gelato

Gelato operates the world's largest print-on-demand network with over 130 production partners in 32 countries. This localized production model means orders are printed and shipped from the facility closest to the customer, reducing shipping times and carbon footprint. Gelato supports apparel, wall art, mugs, phone cases, photo books, and stationery. The platform's pricing is competitive with Printful, and the global production network gives it an edge for stores with international customer bases.

**Pricing**: Free to connect; pay per order based on product and shipping destination.

**Best for**: Stores with global customer bases that need localized production for faster delivery.

---

### 10. DSM Tool

DSM Tool provides multi-channel dropshipping management across Shopify, eBay, and Facebook Marketplace from a single dashboard. The platform supports sourcing from over 50 suppliers and automates listing creation, price monitoring, and order processing across all connected channels. DSM Tool's repricing engine adjusts your prices based on competitor activity and supplier cost changes. The multi-channel approach lets you test products on multiple marketplaces simultaneously.

**Pricing**: $9.99/month for one store; scales based on listings and connected platforms.

**Best for**: Sellers operating across multiple marketplaces who want centralized management.

## How Do You Build a Profitable Dropshipping Store in 2026?

The dropshipping model has matured beyond the "list everything from AliExpress" approach. Profitable stores in 2026 share three characteristics:

**Fast shipping**: Use Spocket, Zendrop, or CJDropshipping's US warehouses for domestic orders under 7 days. Customers now expect Amazon-speed delivery, and 20-day shipping from China kills repeat purchase rates.

**Brand differentiation**: Custom packaging through Zendrop or Printful, branded invoices through Spocket, and curated product selection through Modalyst all create the perception of a real brand rather than a generic dropshipping store.

**Multi-supplier strategy**: Run DSers for AliExpress products with proven demand, Printful for branded custom items, and Spocket for premium fast-shipping products. Diversifying suppliers reduces risk and expands your catalog quality range.

The app you choose determines your operational ceiling. Start with a free plan, validate product-market fit, and invest in premium tools only after you have consistent order volume.
