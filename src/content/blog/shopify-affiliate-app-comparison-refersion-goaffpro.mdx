The best Shopify affiliate apps in 2026 come down to three names: Refersion, UpPromote, and GoAffPro. Each serves a different store size and budget — picking the wrong one means either overpaying for features you will never use or hitting tracking limits exactly when your affiliate channel starts gaining traction.

This comparison breaks down every major dimension: pricing, tracking accuracy, commission flexibility, fraud detection, and who each app is actually built for.

## Best Shopify Affiliate Apps at a Glance

Before going deep, here is the summary table for brands that already know their constraints:

| App | Starting Price | Affiliates Included | Server-Side Tracking | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| GoAffPro | Free / $24/mo Premium | Unlimited | Premium plan only | New stores, bootstrap launches |

The price gap between GoAffPro and Refersion is roughly 4x at the entry level. That gap is mostly justified by tracking infrastructure, fraud tooling, and ecosystem integrations — not by core commission logic, which all three apps handle well.

## Refersion: Deep Tracking, Premium Price Tag

Refersion has been the category leader since 2014 and remains the default choice for brands that have had tracking problems with cheaper apps. Its core advantage is a mature first-party tracking stack that minimizes the attribution gaps created by iOS privacy changes and browser ITP restrictions.

### What Refersion Does Well

**First-party tracking by default.** Refersion routes conversion data through Shopify's native checkout hooks rather than relying on browser cookies. Brands migrating from cookie-only tools often see 10-20% more attributed conversions after switching.

**Publisher marketplace.** Refersion's built-in marketplace surfaces your program to pre-vetted affiliates. For brands that do not want to cold-recruit, this cuts time-to-first-affiliate from weeks to days.

**Integrations.** Native connectors to Klaviyo, Attentive, PostScript, Gorgias, and most major Shopify subscription apps (Recharge, Bold Subscriptions). Subscription commission tracking — where an affiliate earns on the initial sale plus recurring charges — works out of the box on Refersion, whereas the other two require workarounds.

**Fraud detection.** Refersion flags duplicate IPs, suspicious conversion velocity, and self-referral patterns automatically. At $99/month, you get baseline fraud tooling that would cost hundreds more to replicate with GoAffPro add-ons.

### Refersion Pricing Breakdown

- Basic: $99/month — up to 50 active affiliates, core tracking, marketplace access
- Professional: $149/month — up to 200 active affiliates, advanced reporting, custom commission structures
- Enterprise: Custom — unlimited affiliates, dedicated CSM, API access, SLA

The 50-affiliate ceiling on the Basic plan is the most common complaint. A store that recruits aggressively through Instagram DMs can hit this ceiling in the first 30 days. Budget for the Professional plan from the start if you plan to run any kind of active outreach.

### Refersion Verdict

Ideal for brands already generating $30K+ per month in affiliate-driven revenue, brands that need subscription commission tracking, or agencies managing affiliate programs for multiple clients. The price is high relative to alternatives, but tracking accuracy and support quality justify it once you are at scale. For a detailed look at setting up the program itself, see our [Shopify affiliate program setup guide](/blog/shopify-affiliate-program-setup-guide).

## UpPromote: The Feature-Rich Middle Ground

UpPromote (by Secomapp) is the closest thing to a full-featured affiliate platform at a mid-market price point. Its Shopify App Store rating consistently sits above 4.9 with over 4,000 reviews — an unusually strong signal for a B2B SaaS tool where churned customers almost never leave reviews.

### What UpPromote Does Well

**Multi-level commissions.** UpPromote supports two-tier structures where an affiliate earns on their own sales plus a smaller cut from affiliates they recruit — useful for ambassador programs where top performers want to build their own teams.

**Auto-approval and onboarding flows.** Configure rules to auto-approve affiliates based on traffic source or follower count. Approved affiliates are dropped into a branded onboarding sequence — welcome email, portal walkthrough, commission terms — without manual setup per affiliate.

**Coupon code + link dual tracking.** Every affiliate gets both a unique referral URL and a unique coupon code by default. When a customer uses the coupon after the referral cookie expires, the sale still attributes correctly — influencer campaigns generate heavy coupon-search behavior that cookie-only trackers miss entirely.

**Built-in affiliate marketplace.** UpPromote runs its own Marketplace where brands can list open programs and affiliates can apply. It is smaller than Refersion's but growing quickly and skews toward Shopify-native content creators and micro-influencers rather than large coupon publishers.

### UpPromote Pricing Breakdown

- Free: Up to 3 affiliates — useful only for testing
- Growth: $29.99/month — unlimited affiliates, most core features, basic fraud detection
- Professional: $89.99/month — advanced commission tiers, priority support, more automation
- Enterprise: $199.99/month — custom portal domain, multi-store, full API, dedicated support

The Growth plan at $29.99 is where most stores should start. The jump to Professional is worth it when you have more than 100 active affiliates and want to implement tier-based commissions or build custom affiliate recruitment funnels.

### UpPromote vs Refersion: Direct Comparison

| Feature | UpPromote Growth ($29.99) | Refersion Basic ($99) |
|---|---|---|
| Active affiliates | Unlimited | 50 |
| Server-side tracking | Yes | Yes |
| Fraud detection | Basic | Advanced |

For stores under $1M in annual affiliate-driven revenue, UpPromote Growth beats Refersion Basic on both price and feature count. The only meaningful loss is advanced fraud detection and native subscription commission tracking.

### UpPromote Verdict

The best default choice for DTC brands in the $0-$5M revenue range running a performance-based affiliate or ambassador program. The price-to-feature ratio is the strongest in the category. ## GoAffPro: The Lean, Low-Cost Option

GoAffPro targets bootstrapped stores and brands just starting affiliate programs. Its free plan is genuinely functional — not a crippled trial — and the $24/month Premium plan unlocks server-side tracking and white-label branding.

### What GoAffPro Does Well

**Unlimited affiliates on the free plan.** Most tools cap affiliates or add per-affiliate fees on free tiers. GoAffPro does not. A store can launch with hundreds of affiliates on day one at zero monthly cost — a genuine option for brands running large ambassador programs with modest per-affiliate volume.

**White-label portal.** The Premium plan lets you host the affiliate portal on your own subdomain with full brand customization. Refersion charges $149+/month for equivalent white-labeling. GoAffPro charges $24/month.

**Simple setup.** The app installs in under 10 minutes. The core workflow — invite affiliates, assign commissions, review pending payouts — is intuitive enough for non-technical store owners.

### GoAffPro Limitations

**Tracking accuracy on the free plan.** The free tier relies on third-party cookie tracking, increasingly unreliable in Safari and Firefox where cookie lifetime can drop to 24 hours. A customer who clicks a link but converts two days later on a different device will likely not be attributed.

**No native fraud detection.** There is no IP deduplication, velocity detection, or return correlation built in. Manual review is the only safeguard — a real risk when top affiliates drive significant payout volume.

**Limited integrations.** Klaviyo and Omnisend are supported, but most advanced email or subscription tool connections require Zapier or custom API work.

### GoAffPro Pricing Breakdown

- Free: Unlimited affiliates, cookie tracking, basic portal
- Premium: $24/month — server-side tracking, white-label portal, priority support, advanced analytics

### GoAffPro Verdict

Best for stores under $50K/month in revenue that want to experiment with affiliate marketing before committing to higher-cost platforms, or brands running large ambassador programs where each affiliate drives modest individual volume. Upgrade to Premium immediately if you go live — the server-side tracking upgrade alone is worth the $24.

## Tracking Architecture: Where the Real Differences Live

The most important technical difference between these apps is not commission logic or portal design — it is how they attribute conversions after a customer clicks an affiliate link.

**Cookie-based tracking** writes a tracking ID to the browser. It is simple, universal, and increasingly unreliable. Safari's ITP caps cookie lifetime at 7 days (or 1 day for cross-site cookies on some configurations). Firefox's Enhanced Tracking Protection blocks many third-party cookies outright. A customer who clicks an affiliate link on mobile and converts on desktop will likely not attribute correctly.

**Server-side or first-party tracking** works by recording the affiliate referral in the Shopify order metadata at checkout — not relying on the browser to preserve a cookie between sessions. Refersion and UpPromote both implement this through Shopify's checkout extensibility hooks. GoAffPro's Premium plan does the same.

**Coupon code tracking** is the most reliable fallback. If a customer searches for "[brand] discount code" after seeing an affiliate post and uses a code at checkout, that attribution is 100% accurate regardless of browser or device. All three apps support this as a parallel tracking channel.

For stores doing serious paid-media attribution work alongside affiliate programs, see [Shopify attribution models explained](/blog/shopify-attribution-models-explained) — the interaction between affiliate last-touch credit and paid media first-touch credit is a common source of double-counting.

## Commission Structure Flexibility

All three apps support percentage-of-sale commissions, flat dollar commissions, and product-specific commission rates. The differences show up in advanced scenarios:

| Commission Type | GoAffPro Free | GoAffPro Premium | UpPromote Growth | Refersion Basic |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Percentage of sale | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Flat dollar amount | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Product-specific rates | No | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Tiered by volume | No | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Multi-level (MLM) | No | No | Yes | No |
| Subscription recurring | No | No | Limited | Yes |
| Lifetime customer commissions | No | No | Yes | Yes |

Lifetime commissions — where an affiliate earns on all future purchases from a customer they referred, not just the first order — are a powerful retention tool for top affiliates but increase commission liability significantly. Model the cost carefully before enabling this feature.

## Choosing the Right App for Your Store

**Choose GoAffPro** if you are under $500K in annual revenue, have a lean affiliate roster with modest individual volumes, and cannot justify $30+ per month until the channel proves itself. Use the Premium plan from launch.

**Choose UpPromote** if you are in the $500K-$5M revenue range, want the strongest feature set for the price, plan to run multi-tier or ambassador programs, or want to list in a growing affiliate marketplace without paying Refersion's prices.

**Choose Refersion** if you are above $5M in revenue, running subscription-heavy products, need deep integrations with enterprise email and loyalty stacks, or your affiliate program has already experienced tracking accuracy problems with cheaper tools.

## Conclusion

The best Shopify affiliate app depends on your stage, not on which app has the most features. GoAffPro removes all cost friction for early-stage testing. UpPromote gives growing DTC brands everything they need at a price that does not cut into margin. Refersion earns its premium positioning at scale through tracking accuracy, ecosystem depth, and support quality.

Start with the free GoAffPro plan or UpPromote's Growth tier depending on your budget. Run dual tracking (link plus coupon code) from day one. Once affiliate revenue exceeds $10K/month, revisit the tracking accuracy question — that is the moment where Refersion's infrastructure starts paying for itself.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the best Shopify affiliate app for a new store?**
GoAffPro's free plan is the easiest starting point — unlimited affiliates, basic tracking, and a branded portal at no cost. Once you hit 200 active affiliates or need fraud detection, UpPromote Growth ($29.99/month) is the logical upgrade before committing to Refersion's $99/month entry price.

**How does Refersion compare to UpPromote for Shopify?**
Refersion wins on tracking depth and ecosystem integrations; UpPromote wins on price-to-feature ratio below $1M in affiliate-driven revenue. UpPromote also includes multi-level commissions and auto-approval workflows that Refersion reserves for higher tiers.

**Does GoAffPro track affiliate sales accurately?**
On the free plan, cookie-based tracking is increasingly unreliable in Safari and Firefox. Upgrade to GoAffPro Premium ($24/month) for server-side tracking — for stores under $50K/month in affiliate revenue, that accuracy level is sufficient.

**What commission rates do Shopify affiliate programs typically pay?**
Most physical-product DTC brands pay 8-12% on net sales. High-margin categories (supplements, beauty, digital products) can sustain 15-25%. All three apps support flat percentage, flat dollar, tiered, and product-specific rate structures.

**Can I use both referral links and coupon codes together?**
Yes. All three apps assign each affiliate a unique link and a unique coupon simultaneously. Dual tracking prevents revenue leakage when a customer clicks a link but searches for a coupon code before checkout, which is common in influencer campaigns.

**In-house affiliate software vs. a network — which is cheaper?**
Affiliate networks like ShareASale charge a 20-30% override on every payout. On $5,000/month in commissions, that adds $1,000-$1,500 in fees. In-house tools like UpPromote or Refersion pay for themselves once affiliate commissions exceed roughly $3,000-$5,000/month.
