A common approach is to use trending products as lead magnets that bring visitors to your store, and then cross-sell or upsell evergreen products with proven year-round demand. If you must choose one, evergreen products are lower risk because demand persists regardless of trends. However, trending products offer faster initial traction because growing search volume means less competition for visibility. Many successful stores maintain 70% evergreen and 30% trending inventory."

Dropshipping gives you the fastest entry since you can list trending products within hours of identifying them. Finding products with growing demand before the market becomes saturated is the single biggest advantage a new [Shopify](https://shopify.pxf.io/c/6318547/3797171/13624) store can have. Trending products attract organic attention through rising search volumes, generate social media interest naturally, and face less established competition than commoditized product categories.

This list of 20 trending products for 2026 is based on analysis of Google Trends data, Amazon bestseller movement, social media engagement metrics, and wholesale supplier order volumes. For each product, you will find the specific data supporting the trend, estimated profit margins, supplier options, and a realistic assessment of the competition level.

## How We Selected These Products

Every product on this list meets four criteria:

1. **Google Trends growth**: Search interest has increased 30%+ over the past 18 months
2. **Commercial viability**: Gross margins of 40%+ are achievable at competitive retail prices
3. **Shippable**: Products can be economically shipped through standard carriers
4. **Manageable competition**: The market is not yet dominated by major retailers or Amazon private labels

Products are organized by category for easy navigation.

## Health and Wellness Products

### 1. Red Light Therapy Devices (Panels and Wands)

**Why it is trending**: Red light therapy has moved from clinical settings to home use, driven by endorsements from dermatologists and fitness influencers. Google Trends shows a 65% increase in search interest since early 2025. The aging population and growing interest in non-invasive skin treatments drive sustained demand.

**Estimated margins**: 45-60%. Sourcing costs range from $15-80 depending on device size, with retail prices of $39-199.

**Supplier options**: Alibaba manufacturers offer white-label red light panels starting at $15-25 per unit for orders of 100+. CJ Dropshipping lists portable red light wands for $12-20 with dropshipping fulfillment.

**Competition level**: Moderate. Several established brands exist (Joovv, Platinum LED), but the home-use portable segment is still growing and has room for new entrants with competitive pricing and better marketing.

**Best angle for new sellers**: Focus on portable, targeted devices (face wands, hand-held panels) rather than competing with full-body panels. Price under $50 to capture the entry-level buyer.

### 2. Mushroom Coffee and Adaptogen Blends

**Why it is trending**: Functional mushroom supplements like lion's mane, reishi, and chaga blended with coffee have seen a 78% search increase over the past two years. The broader adaptogen and nootropics market is projected to grow 12% annually through 2028.

**Estimated margins**: 55-70%. Mushroom coffee blends cost $4-8 per unit to produce or source in bulk, with retail prices of $24-45 per bag.

**Supplier options**: Private label manufacturers on Alibaba offer custom mushroom coffee blends with minimum orders of 500-1,000 units at $4-7 per unit including packaging. US-based private label companies like Supplement Manufacturing Partners offer lower MOQs at higher per-unit costs.

**Competition level**: Growing but not saturated. Major players like Four Sigmatic and Mud/Wtr have established brands, but the category is expanding fast enough to support new entrants, especially those targeting specific niches like keto-friendly or organic-only blends.

**Best angle for new sellers**: Private label your own blend with a specific audience angle (e.g., "for creative professionals" or "pre-workout clarity blend"). Custom branding differentiates you from generic resellers.

### 3. Portable Massage Guns (Mini/Travel Size)

**Why it is trending**: While full-size massage guns peaked in 2022-2023, the mini/travel segment is experiencing a secondary growth wave. Google Trends shows "mini massage gun" searches up 42% year-over-year. The appeal is portability: fitting in a gym bag, desk drawer, or carry-on luggage.

**Estimated margins**: 50-65%. Mini massage guns cost $8-15 wholesale, retailing for $29-59.

**Supplier options**: AliExpress and CJ Dropshipping offer extensive selections starting at $8-12 per unit. Alibaba manufacturers offer custom branding for orders of 200+ units at $6-10 per unit.

**Competition level**: Moderate-high for generic models, low for differentiated positioning (specific sport focus, premium materials, unique design).

**Best angle for new sellers**: Target specific use cases rather than general fitness. "Massage gun for runners," "desk worker tension relief," or "travel-sized recovery tool" narrow the competition while capturing dedicated audiences.

### 4. Sleep Optimization Products

**Why it is trending**: Sleep health has become a mainstream wellness category. Products like weighted sleep masks, mouth tape for nasal breathing, blue light blocking glasses specifically designed for evening use, and cooling pillow inserts have all seen 30-50% search increases.

**Estimated margins**: 60-75%. Many sleep products have very low COGS ($1-5) with retail prices of $12-35.

**Supplier options**: AliExpress carries most sleep accessories at $1-5 per unit. Alibaba offers bulk pricing with custom branding for $0.50-3 per unit depending on product.

**Competition level**: Low to moderate for niche sleep products. The broad "sleep products" category is competitive, but specific items like mouth tape or weighted eye masks have fewer dedicated sellers.

**Best angle for new sellers**: Create a curated "sleep kit" bundling 3-4 complementary products. Bundles increase average order value and differentiate you from single-product competitors.

## Home and Lifestyle Products

### 5. Desk Treadmills and Under-Desk Ellipticals

**Why it is trending**: The permanent shift to hybrid and remote work has sustained demand for movement solutions that integrate with workstations. "Under desk treadmill" searches have grown 55% over the past 18 months, with no sign of slowing.

**Estimated margins**: 35-50%. Under-desk treadmills wholesale for $80-150, retailing for $199-349.

**Supplier options**: Alibaba manufacturers offer white-label options at $80-120 per unit for orders of 50-100 units. Under-desk ellipticals wholesale for $30-60 with retail prices of $79-149.

**Competition level**: Moderate. Several brands are established, but the category is large enough to support niche positioning.

**Best angle for new sellers**: The under-desk elliptical sub-category has less competition and lower shipping costs than treadmills. Target remote workers specifically with workspace-integrated marketing.

### 6. Smart Home Fragrance Devices

**Why it is trending**: Automated scent diffusers controlled by smartphone apps represent the intersection of smart home technology and wellness. Searches for "smart diffuser" and "smart home fragrance" are up 48% year-over-year.

**Estimated margins**: 50-65%. Smart diffusers wholesale for $10-25, retailing for $35-79.

**Supplier options**: Shenzhen manufacturers on Alibaba offer white-label smart diffusers with app control for $12-20 per unit at 200+ MOQ. AliExpress offers dropshipping options at $15-28 per unit.

**Competition level**: Low-moderate. The category is emerging and has relatively few dedicated Shopify sellers.

**Best angle for new sellers**: Sell the diffuser alongside proprietary essential oil blends for recurring revenue. The device is the acquisition tool; the consumable oils are the profit engine.

### 7. Modular and Space-Saving Furniture

**Why it is trending**: Urban living in smaller spaces continues driving demand for furniture that transforms, folds, or serves multiple functions. "Space saving furniture" searches are up 38% over the past year.

**Estimated margins**: 40-55% on smaller items. Wall-mounted desks, folding tables, and storage ottomans wholesale for $25-80 with retail prices of $59-199.

**Supplier options**: Alibaba manufacturers offer extensive catalogs of space-saving furniture. Shipping costs for furniture are significant, so prioritize lighter, flat-pack items. Spocket offers some US-based furniture suppliers.

**Competition level**: Moderate. Amazon dominates the lower end, but curated, design-focused Shopify stores can compete on aesthetics and brand experience.

**Best angle for new sellers**: Focus on one room (e.g., "small kitchen solutions" or "studio apartment furniture") rather than all space-saving furniture. Niche focus makes your store the authority.

### 8. Portable Power Stations and Solar Generators

**Why it is trending**: Grid reliability concerns, outdoor recreation growth, and van life culture sustain demand for portable power. "Portable power station" searches are up 35% over 18 months, accelerating during storm seasons.

**Estimated margins**: 30-45%. Portable power stations wholesale for $60-200 depending on capacity, with retail prices of $149-499.

**Supplier options**: Major manufacturers like Jackery and Bluetti sell through authorized reseller programs. Unbranded units from Alibaba manufacturers cost $60-150 for 500Wh-1000Wh capacity.

**Competition level**: High for major brands, moderate for unbranded/white-label options targeting specific use cases.

**Best angle for new sellers**: Focus on specific audiences: van life enthusiasts, apartment preppers, outdoor photographers, or RV owners. Package with educational content about power needs calculations.

## Personal Care and Beauty

### 9. Scalp Care Products (Massagers, Serums, Scrubs)

**Why it is trending**: "Scalp care" has emerged as its own beauty sub-category, distinct from hair care. Scalp massagers, exfoliating scrubs, and scalp serums have seen 52% search growth. The trend parallels the earlier skincare movement, applied to the scalp.

**Estimated margins**: 60-75%. Scalp massagers cost $1-3 wholesale, retailing for $12-24. Private label scalp serums cost $3-7 per unit, retailing for $18-35.

**Supplier options**: AliExpress for electric and manual scalp massagers at $2-8 per unit. Private label cosmetic manufacturers for serums and scrubs.

**Competition level**: Low-moderate. The category is growing faster than competition is entering.

**Best angle for new sellers**: Create a "scalp care routine" bundle with a massager, scrub, and serum. Position as the complete scalp health solution.

### 10. Waterless Beauty Products (Powder Cleansers, Solid Moisturizers)

**Why it is trending**: Sustainability-conscious consumers are adopting waterless beauty formulations that reduce packaging, shipping weight, and environmental impact. Searches for "powder cleanser" and "solid moisturizer" are up 44% year-over-year.

**Estimated margins**: 65-80%. Waterless beauty products are lightweight, inexpensive to ship, and command premium pricing due to their eco-friendly positioning.

**Supplier options**: US-based private label cosmetic manufacturers offer small-batch production. Alibaba has overseas options at lower costs but requires careful quality verification.

**Competition level**: Low. This is an emerging category with few dedicated Shopify stores. Most competition comes from established clean beauty brands.

**Best angle for new sellers**: Lead with the sustainability angle and target eco-conscious consumers through environmentally focused content marketing.

### 11. At-Home Teeth Whitening Kits

**Why it is trending**: Professional teeth whitening costs $300-1,000 per session, driving consistent demand for at-home alternatives. LED whitening kits searches remain strong with steady 30% year-over-year growth.

**Competition level**: Moderate-high. Many sellers exist, but the market is large enough to support new entrants with strong branding and social proof.

**Best angle for new sellers**: Focus on the refill model. Sell kits at competitive prices and build recurring revenue through gel refill subscriptions.

## How to Act on These Trends

### Step 1: Validate Before Committing

Even with strong trend data, validate each product using the methods outlined in our product validation guide. Order samples, test ads, and confirm demand with your specific target audience before investing significantly.

### Step 2: Start With Dropshipping or POD

Test trending products with zero inventory risk. List them on your [Shopify](https://shopify.pxf.io/c/6318547/3797171/13624) store through a dropshipping app and drive a small amount of targeted traffic. Products that generate sales graduate to bulk sourcing for better margins.

### Step 3: Build a Brand, Not Just a Product Listing

The merchants who sustain profitability in trending categories are those who build brands around them, not those who simply list generic products. Custom packaging, original content, and an authentic brand story create defensibility against competitors who will inevitably enter the market.

### Step 4: Plan for the Trend Lifecycle

Every trend has a lifecycle: emergence, growth, peak, maturity, and decline. Enter during emergence or growth, maximize profit during growth and peak, and have your next product ready before decline begins. Use Google Trends to monitor where each product sits on this curve.
